  Program Features & Benefits

Disclaimer: All sample data shown in the screenshots below are based on a fictional client.

Loan Type Decision Tree

The NSLDS Loan Type to Debt Resolution Decision Tree. Take the guess work out of the equation.

Repayment Options Calculator

If your best option is a typical repayment, you may qualify, based on your income and several other data points, for Income Based Repayment with a floor of $5.00. Or you could qualify for an Income Contingent Repayment Plan. Loan repayment amount vary based on loan balnaced and weighted interest rates. There are lots of options. Know Yours.

National Student Loan Data System (NSLDS) Integration

Analyze the Student Loan Data file and extract meaningful visual guided step-by-step directions for all qualified programs.

Detailed Solution Descriptions

The software is designed to match up solutions with each loan type and provide detailed explanation and analysis of each solution type and sub category. Did you know each Loan Type has 9 solution main categories and 19 sub categories? That's a lot of options. Find yours!

Detailed Financial Summary & Budget

Income, Expenses and the usual budget items. Several Forbearance, Postponement and Forgiveness options require the software to calculate the percentage of disposable income.

Find a Solution for Every Debt

Know your options and leave no stone unturned. We look at every possible outcome based on a variety of data points. See if you qualify for a simple Forberance or an Ability to Benefit Cancellation. We've got you covered.

Keep Track of Your Status in the Program

Update your program tasks, send emails, create reminders and prepare documents.
